Registration & Onboarding

What documents are required for farmer registration?

For farmer registration, you need:

  • Valid Kisan Card (12-digit number)
  • Aadhaar Card for identity verification
  • Land ownership documents (7/12 extract or equivalent)
  • Bank account details for payments
  • Recent passport-size photograph

All documents are verified through government databases for authenticity.

How long does registration approval take?

Registration approval timelines vary by user type:

  • Farmers/Coastal Communities: 24-48 hours
  • Corporate Entities: 2-3 business days
  • NGOs/Organizations: 3-5 business days

You'll receive email notifications at each stage of the approval process.

Can I register multiple land parcels under one account?

Yes, you can register multiple land parcels under a single account. After initial registration, you can add additional land parcels through your dashboard by providing:

  • Land survey numbers and location details
  • Ownership documents for each parcel
  • Geotagged photos of the land

Each land parcel will be treated as a separate project for carbon credit calculation.

What if I don't have a Kisan Card?

If you don't have a Kisan Card, you can still register using alternative documents:

  • Aadhaar Card for identity verification
  • Land ownership records (7/12 extract, RoR)
  • Bank account details
  • Panchayat verification certificate

Our support team will guide you through the alternative registration process.

Project Management

What types of projects are eligible for carbon credits?

Eligible projects include:

  • Mangrove Restoration: Planting and maintaining mangrove ecosystems
  • Coastal Afforestation: Tree planting in coastal areas
  • Seagrass Rehabilitation: Restoring seagrass meadows
  • Wetland Conservation: Protecting and enhancing wetlands
  • Salt Marsh Restoration: Rebuilding salt marsh ecosystems

All projects must demonstrate additionality and follow approved methodologies.

How often do I need to upload project data?

Data upload frequency depends on your project phase:

  • Monthly: Progress photos and basic updates
  • Quarterly: Detailed progress reports
  • Annually: Comprehensive monitoring data
  • As needed: Significant changes or events

The platform will send you reminders for upcoming data upload deadlines.

What happens if my project fails or plants die?

We understand that restoration projects can face challenges:

  • Replacement Planting: You can replant failed areas
  • Insurance Buffer: We maintain a 20% buffer for project risks
  • Technical Support: Our agronomists can provide guidance
  • Force Majeure: Natural disasters are accounted for in risk assessment

Transparent reporting of challenges helps us work together on solutions.

Carbon Credits

How are carbon credits calculated?

Carbon credits are calculated using scientific methods:

  • IPCC Methodologies: Following international standards
  • Satellite Monitoring: Regular remote sensing of biomass
  • Field Measurements: Ground truthing and sampling
  • Species-specific Factors: Different plants sequester different amounts
  • Soil Carbon Analysis: Measuring below-ground carbon

1 carbon credit = 1 tonne of CO2 equivalent sequestered.

When will I receive my first carbon credits?

The timeline for receiving carbon credits depends on:

  • Project Establishment: 3-6 months for initial verification
  • First Verification: 12 months for first credit issuance
  • Ongoing Credits: Quarterly after first verification

You can track your credit accumulation in real-time through your dashboard.

Can I sell my carbon credits to anyone?

Yes, within the BlueCarbon India marketplace:

  • Open Marketplace: Sell to any registered corporate buyer
  • Direct Offers: Receive purchase offers from companies
  • Auction: Set minimum price and allow bidding
  • Forward Contracts: Pre-sell future credit generations

All transactions are secure and transparent through blockchain technology.

Payments & Transactions

How and when will I receive payments?

Payment process details:

  • Payment Method: Direct bank transfer to your registered account
  • Processing Time: 7 business days after credit sale
  • Transaction Fee: 5% platform fee deducted from sale amount
  • Payment Tracking: Real-time status in your dashboard

You'll receive SMS and email notifications for all payment transactions.

Are there any hidden charges or fees?

We maintain complete transparency in all charges:

  • Platform Fee: 5% on credit sales (clearly displayed)
  • Verification Cost: Actual third-party verification charges
  • No Hidden Fees: No registration, maintenance, or withdrawal fees
  • GST: Applicable GST as per government regulations

All charges are displayed before you confirm any transaction.

What if there's an issue with my payment?

If you encounter payment issues:

  • Check Bank Details: Ensure your registered bank account is active
  • Contact Support: Reach out to our payment support team
  • Dispute Resolution: File a payment dispute through your dashboard
  • Escalation: Escalate to our grievance officer if unresolved

Most payment issues are resolved within 48 hours.
