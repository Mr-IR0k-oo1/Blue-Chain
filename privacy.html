1. Overview & Commitment

Our Commitment: BlueCarbon India is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data in compliance with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

This Privacy Policy explains how BlueCarbon India ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal data when you use our platform and services.

By using the BlueCarbon India platform, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy from time to time, and we will notify you of any material changes.

Key Definitions
Personal Data: Any data about an individual who is identifiable by or in relation to such data
Data Principal: The individual to whom the personal data relates
Data Fiduciary: BlueCarbon India who determines the purpose and means of processing
Processing: Any operation performed on personal data

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following categories of personal data:

Data Category Examples Purpose
Identity Data Name, Aadhaar, PAN, photo, signature User verification and KYC
Contact Data Email, phone, address, location Communication and service delivery
Financial Data Bank details, transaction history Payment processing and accounting
Technical Data IP address, device info, browser type Platform security and analytics
Project Data Land details, carbon measurements, photos Carbon credit calculation and verification

2.2 Sensitive Personal Data

We may process certain sensitive personal data with your explicit consent:

  • Financial Information: Bank account details, transaction records
  • Official Identifiers: Aadhaar, PAN, other government IDs
  • Biometric Data: For Aadhaar authentication (where applicable)
  • Geolocation Data: Precise location of restoration projects
Note: We only collect sensitive personal data when necessary for regulatory compliance and with your explicit consent.

3. How We Use Your Data

3.1 Primary Purposes

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

User Verification

KYC processes and identity verification

Project Management

Carbon credit calculation and verification

Financial Transactions

Payment processing and accounting

Compliance

Regulatory reporting and legal requirements

3.2 Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: When you explicitly consent to specific processing
  • Contract: To fulfill our contractual obligations to you
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Legitimate Interests: For platform security and improvement

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

4.1 Third-Party Sharing

We may share your personal data with the following categories of recipients:

Recipient Data Shared Purpose
Government Authorities Identity, project data Regulatory compliance and verification
Verification Agencies Project data, location Carbon credit verification
Payment Processors Financial data Transaction processing
Technology Partners Technical data Platform functionality and security

4.2 International Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside India:

  • We ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses
  • Transfers only occur when necessary for service delivery
  • We comply with data localization requirements under Indian law
Data Protection: All third-party partners are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for specified purposes.

5. Data Security & Protection

5.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

Encryption

End-to-end encryption for all data

Access Control

Role-based access and authentication

Network Security

Firewalls and intrusion detection

Audit Trails

Comprehensive activity logging

5.2 Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Duration of account activity + 5 years
  • Financial Records: 7 years as per tax laws
  • Project Data: 30 years for carbon credit verification
  • Inactive Accounts: 2 years after last activity

6. Your Rights Under DPDPA 2023

6.1 Data Principal Rights

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights:

Right Description How to Exercise
Right to Access Access your personal data and processing details Submit access request through platform
Right to Correction Correct inaccurate or incomplete data Update profile or request correction
Right to Erasure Delete personal data (with limitations) Submit erasure request
Right to Grievance Redressal Register complaints about data processing Contact Data Protection Officer
Right to Nominate Nominate someone to exercise rights on your behalf Submit nomination request

6.2 Exercising Your Rights

To exercise your rights, you can:

  • Use the self-service options in your account dashboard
  • Contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@bluecarbon.gov.in
  • Submit a formal request through our grievance portal
Response Time: We will respond to all valid requests within 30 days as required by law.

7. Compliance & Governance

7.1 Legal Compliance

BlueCarbon India complies with all applicable data protection laws:

  • Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023
  • Information Technology Act, 2000 and Rules
  • Reserve Bank of India guidelines for payment data
  • Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change regulations
