About BlueCarbon India

Revolutionizing Coastal Ecosystem Restoration through Blockchain Technology

Our Mission

To create a transparent, efficient, and equitable platform that connects coastal communities with global carbon markets, enabling sustainable ecosystem restoration while generating economic opportunities.

BlueCarbon India is a Ministry of Earth Sciences initiative that leverages cutting-edge blockchain technology, satellite monitoring, and community participation to create India's premier blue carbon credit ecosystem.

Guiding principles that drive our mission

Transparency

How BlueCarbon India Works

A seamless process from restoration to revenue generation

Register & Verify

Coastal communities register with Kisan Card and land verification

Implement Projects

Start restoration activities with technical guidance and support

Monitor & Verify

Satellite and drone monitoring with third-party verification

Earn & Trade

Receive carbon credits and trade in transparent marketplace

Our Journey

Key milestones in developing India's blue carbon ecosystem

2022 - Concept Development

Initial research and concept development for blockchain-based carbon credit platform under MoES guidance.

2023 Q1 - Platform Development

Development of core platform with blockchain integration and satellite monitoring capabilities.

2023 Q3 - Pilot Launch

Successful pilot launch in Sundarbans with 25 coastal communities and 3 corporate partners.

2024 Q1 - National Expansion

Platform expansion to 6 coastal states with 100+ projects and 50+ corporate buyers.

2024 Q2 - Current Status

127 active projects, 45,620 carbon credits generated, and ₹2.3Cr revenue for communities.
