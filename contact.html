Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does registration approval take?

Registration approval typically takes 24-48 hours for individual farmers and 2-3 business days for corporate and NGO registrations after all documents are submitted.

What documents are required for farmer registration?

Farmers need Kisan Card, Aadhaar Card, land ownership documents, and bank account details. All documents are verified through government databases.

How are carbon credits calculated?

Carbon credits are calculated using IPCC-approved methodologies based on satellite monitoring, drone surveys, and field measurements of carbon sequestration.

When are payments processed?

Payments are processed within 7 days of credit sale completion. You can track payment status in your dashboard.

Is there mobile app support?

Yes, we have a mobile app available on Android and iOS with offline capabilities for data collection in remote areas.
